Taoiseach Remains Silent as Trump’s Comments Stir Debate on Ireland’s Airspace Defence

Former U.S. President Donald Trump criticized Ireland’s airspace defence, calling it a “total disaster” and offering to help improve it. He referenced retired military pilot Kevin Phipps, who had expressed similar concerns. Despite the controversy, Taoiseach Micheál Martin has yet to comment on the issue. The debate over Ireland’s air defence continues as the government prepares for future decisions.
